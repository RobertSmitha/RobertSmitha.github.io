About Yia Yia's Greek Express

The family-owned Greek restaurant Yia Yia's Greek Express opened in May 2016 and serves authentic Greek food from recipes that have been passed down in the family for generations.

By taking pride in our establishment and preparing our dishes to perfection, we create an atmosphere that will leave you feeling like a Greek God.

Finally, for the first time, you do not have to drive all the way down to Tarpon Springs for some authentic Greek food!
