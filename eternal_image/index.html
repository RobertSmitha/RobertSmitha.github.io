YOUR PROCESS

Booking

After we get to know you and your wedding plans we move forward with the booking process by finalizing a package. We draft a contract and send it to you via email for you to print, review and sign. Make a copy for your records and then send the competed contract and your retainer to our studio via letter or scanned images. Our contracts expire seven days after being sent out so please make sure to be prepared to sign when you request a booking with us. Any weddings outside of the Tallahassee, FL area are subject to travel and lodging fees. Sales tax is determined by the location the final product will be shipped to. Once we receive your signed contract and deposit, we will send you a confirmation and congratulate you on officially booking with us.

Pre-Production

Next we send you a list of important tips for getting the best wedding cinematography possible, and also ask that you send us a schedule of the day. We understand that final drafts of itineraries and scheduled or subject to change, so we usually like to give you a quick call a few days before the wedding to catch up and make sure everything is set.

Day of Wedding

We start our day of shooting usually during makeup at bridal prep. If we have two shooters, we will split up to capture both the bride and groom preps. We love when our couples have first looks and will use both shooters to capture it in a creative way. Our team will capture the ceremony using several mics, one for the groom, one for the officiant, and one set up near the front of the venue. These mics are removed immediately after the conclusion of the ceremony. During the reception, when lights are dimmed or turned off, we use high powered LED lights to ensure that our cameras are able to capture every part of the reception.

Post-Wedding

We begin working on your cinematic wedding trailer in the weeks immediately following your wedding date. The trailer is usually ready in about 2 weeks, and is sent to you via YouTube and a wetransfer.com download email. We encourage our couples to post the video on social media so friends and family can see. The editing process is more lengthy, taking between 2-3 months to complete; we finalize your custom blu-ray discs, and prepare your archive discs and hard drives. The final product is sent via post.
