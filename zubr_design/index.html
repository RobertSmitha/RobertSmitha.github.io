About

Real interior design.

  • Meet the Designer

    Wanda Zubr

    Ms. Zubr is a professional interior designer with an eclectic style. With 30+ years of experience in the United States and internationally, she has mastered a variety of styles and is capable of meeting any and all design requirements.

  • Education

    Interior Design & Environmental Design Bachelor's Degree

    During her time at Parsons School of Design, Ms. Zubr earned her Bachelor's of Art in Interior and Environmental Design. The rigorous, creative and prestigious work ethic Wanda developed at Parsons will be forever engraved in her design style.

  • Experience

    Work in the field

    Ms. Zubr has been in a multitude of administrative and managerial positions. Wanda's extensive design experience has been reinforced again and again throughout her career. She has also held many distinguished positions in organizations all over the world.

  • And more..

    By incorporating her vast design experience, Ms. Zubr has directed a broad variety of design projects. She has developed strong design, administrative, financial and organizational skills throughout her career and these values reflect in her work.
